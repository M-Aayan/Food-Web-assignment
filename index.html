Menu:

Karahi:

Karahi is a sizzling South Asian delight, with tender meat cooked in a rich blend of spices, tomatoes, and green chilies. This aromatic dish bursts with bold flavors, balanced by fresh ginger, yogurt, and cilantro. Served with naan or rice, it’s a must-try for lovers of authentic, hearty cuisine.



Pizza:

Pizza is a beloved classic, featuring a crispy crust topped with rich tomato sauce, melted cheese, and a variety of savory toppings. With flavors ranging from smoky pepperoni to fresh basil and veggies, every bite is a perfect blend of gooey, crispy, and deliciously satisfying. It’s a must for anyone craving comforting, irresistible flavors!



Smash Burger:

A smash burger is a mouthwatering twist on the classic, with a juicy beef patty pressed flat on a hot griddle for crispy edges and a tender center. Topped with melted cheese, fresh lettuce, tomato, and a zesty sauce, each bite delivers a perfect balance of savory, crunchy, and juicy. It’s a burger lover’s dream in every delicious layer!




Our Special:

Alfredo Pasta:

Alfredo pasta is a creamy, comforting dish featuring tender pasta coated in a rich sauce of butter, cream, and Parmesan cheese. Each bite is luxuriously smooth, with a hint of garlic and a sprinkle of fresh parsley, making it irresistible for anyone who loves indulgent, velvety flavors. Perfect for a cozy, satisfying meal!



Chesse Handi:

Cheese handi is a rich and indulgent South Asian dish, combining succulent pieces of chicken or meat cooked in a creamy, spiced tomato gravy with generous amounts of melted cheese. The thick, velvety sauce, infused with aromatic spices, pairs perfectly with naan or rice. It’s a must-try for anyone craving bold, cheesy goodness in every bite!
